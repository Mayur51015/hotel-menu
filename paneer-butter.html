Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan, add ginger-garlic paste, and saut√©.
  2. Add tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Pour in fresh cream and stir.
  5. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
