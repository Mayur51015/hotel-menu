Chicken Afghani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1/2 cup hung curd
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with curd, cream, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, black pepper, lemon juice, and salt for 2-3 hours.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and grill the marinated chicken until cooked.
  3. Turn occasionally for even cooking and a smoky flavor.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
