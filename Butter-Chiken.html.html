Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (boneless, cut into pieces)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with salt, turmeric, and chili powder for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat butter in a pan, add ginger-garlic paste, and saut√©.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until golden.
  4. Pour in tomato puree, cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add cream, garam masala, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
