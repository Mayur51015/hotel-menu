Veg Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • 2 boiled potatoes, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Green chutney
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread butter on each slice of bread.
  2. Apply a layer of green chutney on one side of each bread slice.
  3. Place slices of potato, cucumber, and tomato over the chutney layer.
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cover with another slice of bread and press gently.
  6. Cut into halves and serve with ketchup or additional chutney.
