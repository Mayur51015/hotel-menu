Poha Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flattened rice (Poha)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the poha in water and drain it completely. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them pop.
  3. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the poha and stir gently. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and squeeze lemon juice.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
